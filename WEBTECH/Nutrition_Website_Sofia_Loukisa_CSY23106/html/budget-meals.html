CHEESE PIE


Portion 4 meals


Time 35 minutes


  • 1 puff pastry sheet
  • 200 g cream cheese
  • 100 g feta cheese

Execution Method


Preheat the oven to 190°C (370° F) set to fan. Place a piece of parchment paper on your worktop, spread the puff pastry sheet over it, and press the puff pastry down with a rolling pin to flatten it. Place the cream cheese over half of the puff pastry sheet and spread it with a spoon. Crumble the feta cheese with your hands, scatter it over the cream cheese, and cover it with the other half of the puff pastry. Press the edges of the puff pastry with your fingers in order to seal the filling well, and use a fork to prick the whole surface of the puff pastry. Transfer the pie to a baking pan, put the pan in the oven, and bake the cheese pie for 25-30 minutes at the top oven rack position.



To serve


Remove the pan from the oven and serve.
