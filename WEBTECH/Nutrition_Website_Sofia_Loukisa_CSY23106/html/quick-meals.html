ARRABBIATA PASTA


Portion 4 meals


Time 15 minutes


  • 500 g fusilli pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 2 clove(s) of garlic
  • 3-4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon(s) chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoon(s) tomato paste
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 1 kilo water
  • 1 tablespoon(s) granulated sugar
  • salt
  • pepper

Execution Method


Finely chop the onion and the garlic, and set them aside. Place a pot over high heat, add the olive oil, onion, and garlic, and mix them with a wooden spoon. Add the chili flakes and mix again. Add the tomato paste and saut√© it for 2-3 minutes. Add the canned tomatoes, water, pasta, salt, pepper, and sugar. Sti well and boil the pasta according to the packet's instructions. Remove the pot from the heat and mix the pasta well. Divide it among plates and serve with grated parmesan and parsley.



To serve


Parmesan cheese, grated and parsley, fresh, finely chopped
