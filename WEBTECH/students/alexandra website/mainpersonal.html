Computer Science

My digital passion.


Computing science became my passion once I realised what I am able to create with it. My creativity can be limitless inside this endless world of binary numbers.

As an individual, I believe travelling is required for an individuals growth. Therefore I enjoy travelling, and I do travel often. This profession, computer science, allows me to work remotely, from wherever in the world I please.

Therefore, it allows me to expand my horizons and never run out of inspiration, while coming up with new creative ideas.
After all, changing out enviroment is key to remaining productive.

The combinition of unlimited "space" to create and being able to create anything from nothing, along with the ability to travel to prevent burn-out, really drew me to this profession.

That aside, coding is one of the most enjoyable things I have found myself commiting to.
No matter how tough and frustrating some codes might get to create, I enjoy the whole process.
Concluding, I believe this is the most creative profession one can have and I absolutely love it.
